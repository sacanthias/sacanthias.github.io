## What It Is
Sheep Protector is a single-player environmental adventure game, where the player dons the role of a sheepdog attempting to guide a single sheep through a treacherous forest back to the main herd. Along the way, the two encounter obstacles and hostile wildlife, which they must avoid to return home.

# Development
Sheep Protector is still currently in development. We plan to display our game at RIT's [Interactive Media Symposium](www.rit.edu/computing/school-interactive-games-and-media/ims).

Some of the work I've done with Sheep Protector involve the designing and implementation of various menus in the game, such as the start screen and pause menus, the design and process of which I discuss more in depth in [this](@/blog/menus.md) blog post.
