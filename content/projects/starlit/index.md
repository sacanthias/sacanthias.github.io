Starlit Wanderer was a personal project developed for the Granola.gg 2024 Game Jam, based on the theme 'nostalgia.'
<!-- more -->

In Starlit Wanderer, the player acts as an astronaut adrift in space and, in their final moments, they think back on their childhood.



In this 48-hour game jam, I was responsible for asset production, which included in-game sprites and resources, cutscenes, game backgrounds, and background patterns. As the main visual artist, my role also involved communicating with my other teammates to deliver assets that accurately reflected our narrative and were relevant to gameplay.

All assets were made in Aseprite.
