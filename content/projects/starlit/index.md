Starlit Wanderer was a personal project developed for the Granola.gg 2024 Game Jam, based on the theme 'nostalgia.'
<!-- more -->

In this 48-hour game jam, I was responsible for asset production, which included in-game sprites and resources, cutscenes, game backgrounds, and background patterns. 

My role also involved communicating with my other teammates to deliver assets that accurately reflected our narrative .

All assets were made in Aseprite.
