    What It Is

    Sheep Protector (working title) is a single-player environmental adventure game, where the player dons the role of a sheepdog attempting to guide a single sheep through a treacherous forest back to the main herd. Along the way, the two encounter obstacles and hostile wildlife, which they must avoid to return home.

    Development

    Sheep Protector is still currently in development. We plan to display our game at RIT's Interactive Media Symposium.
